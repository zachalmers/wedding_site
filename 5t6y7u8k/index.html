Travel

One stop away from most major US cities, Oaxaca is easy to reach with a bit of planning.

Flights

Best option: fly directly into Oaxaca International Airport (OAX). If needed, route via Mexico City (MEX) and connect onward.

Mexico City is one of our favorite big cities in the world. Break up the journy and some time there if you can. Pro Tip: Centurion lounge at MEX makes a mean carajilo.

Getting around

Most guests will be comfortable using rideshare/taxis within the city. For events outside town, we’ll provide transportation details closer to the date. For airport transfer details, click here.

Althought there is no Uber in Oaxaca, DiDi is widely used and reliable. Download the app in advance and set up your account.

Practical notes

Weather

December in Oaxaca is sunny and mild. Expect warm days and cooler evenings-bring a light jacket or shawl.

Altitude

Oaxaca sits at a 5,000 ft in elevation. Take it easy on day one, drink water (not mezcal), and keep snacks handy.

Safety

Oaxaca is generally safe and welcoming. Use normal city awareness and consider rideshare at night for longer journeys.

Kids

Kids are welcome. If you need childcare suggestions, please reach out closer to the event.
